About the Project
What is EduTech Impact on AI?

This platform analyzes whether AI study tools (like ChatGPT, Copilot, and similar) genuinely improve student performance — across every level of education from middle school to post-graduation.

Teachers and administrators can upload assessment data, explore interactive dashboards, filter by subject and class, and even use our trained machine learning model to predict a student's expected score.

  • 📤 Upload CSV/Excel student assessment data
  • 📊 View 7+ interactive charts and KPI cards
  • 🤖 Predict scores using the AI model
  • ⬇️ Export filtered data as CSV
  • 👥 Multi-user with admin controls

Platform Features
Everything in One Dashboard
From raw data upload to AI-powered predictions — built as a complete full-stack analytics system.
📤

Smart Data Upload

Upload CSV or Excel files with automatic validation. Bad rows are skipped and reported — your upload never crashes.

📊

Live Analytics Dashboard

7 interactive Chart.js charts that update in real time when filters change — no page reloads needed.

🔍

Advanced Filters

Filter every chart by subject, class, AI tool usage, and date range. All charts update simultaneously.

🤖

AI Predictive Model

Linear Regression model (R²=0.79) predicts a student's expected score from study hours, AI usage, and subject.

⬇️

Export to CSV

Download filtered and pipeline-enriched data as CSV — includes score bands, AI groups, and trend columns.

👥

Multi-User Auth

Login system with teacher and admin roles. Admins can manage users, activate/deactivate accounts, and change roles.

⏱

Auto-Refresh

Toggle auto-refresh to poll for new data every 30 seconds. Dashboard updates silently when new uploads arrive.

🏥

Pipeline Health Panel

Real-time view of data quality — mean score, score range, outlier count, AI adoption rate, and date range.

🏆

Student Leaderboard

Top 5 and bottom 5 students by average score, with AI tool usage badge and medal rankings.

How It Works
4 Simple Steps
From your spreadsheet to actionable AI insights in minutes.
1

Login or Register

Create a teacher account or sign in with your own secure credentials. Your session is secure and persistent.

2

Upload Your Data

Upload a CSV or Excel file with student records. Required: student_id, class, subject, score, AI tool used, study hours, date.

3

Explore the Dashboard

Charts populate instantly. Use filters to drill into specific subjects, classes, or date ranges. Export data anytime.

4

Run AI Predictions

Train the model on your data, then enter study hours + subject + AI usage to get a predicted score with confidence band.

🔬 Key Research Finding

+9.2

Students who use AI study tools score an average of 9.2 points higher than those who don't — across all subjects and education levels. AI tool usage is the single strongest predictor of student score in our model.
